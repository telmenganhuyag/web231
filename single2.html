MONTE, THEMONGOLZ CLINCH ESL CHALLENGER ATLANTA PLAYOFFS; COMPLEXITY ELIMINATED

Posted By Telmen    On 17 Dec, 2023    Comments(70)

The American team crumbled in their group decider and head home in disappointment.

The playoff bracket for championship Sunday is set in Atlanta after TheMongolz and Monte secured victory in their group decider matches over Complexity and BESTIA, respectively.

TheMongolz started their tournament run off on good footing after narrowly besting Complexity in an overtime affair, but couldn't book an early ticket to the playoffs after suffering a one-sided loss to Apeks.

Their second go at Complexity went even better, however, with the Mongolian roster blowing Johnny "⁠JT⁠" Theodosiou's side out of the server on Inferno (13-3) before capitalizing on numerous mistakes to end the IEM Sydney runner-ups' eight-map winstreak on Overpass (13-8).
