THEMONGOLZ QUALIFY FOR ESL CHALLENGER ATLANTA

Posted By Telmen    On 18 Dec, 2023    Comments(50)

The Mongolian squad took the penultimate spot at the $100,000 LAN event.

They will join teams such as Complexity, Monte, and Virtus.pro in Atlanta between December 15-17 as eight teams are set to battle it out for a $100,000 prize pool and a spot at ESL Pro League Season 19.

TheMongolz were directly invited to the closed stage of the qualifier alongside fellow grand finalists TYLOO. The game was a rematch of their previous encounter in the ESL Challenger Jönköping qualifier, where TYLOO prevailed to secure their spot at the Swedish event, but the Chinese side couldn't repeat the feat this time around.

The final started on Nuke, and TYLOO got off to a hot start on their defense to go 8-2 up before a double-AWP setup by TheMongolz netted them four at the switch. The Mongolian squad kept up their momentum and tied up the scores at nine, but Zhuo "⁠advent⁠" Liang's 1vs2 clutch put his team up to map point, which they converted to a 13-11 victory.
